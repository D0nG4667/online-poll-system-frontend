# User Stories

## üé≠ Roles
-   **Creator**: A registered user who creates and manages polls.
-   **Voter**: A guest or registered user who participates in polls.
-   **System**: The backend/AI services processing data.

## üöÄ Epic: Poll Management

### Story 1: Create Poll
**As a** Creator,  
**I want to** easily create a new poll with a title, description, and multiple questions,  
**So that** I can gather specific feedback from my audience.

### Story 2: Edit/Delete Poll
**As a** Creator,  
**I want to** edit or delete my polls that are in "Draft" status,  
**So that** I can correct mistakes before publishing.

### Story 3: Manage My Polls
**As a** Creator,  
**I want to** view a paginated list of all my polls with their status (Active/Closed),  
**So that** I can navigate through my history without overwhelming the page.

---

## üì¢ Epic: Distribution & Sharing

### Story 4: Share via Link
**As a** Creator,  
**I want to** get a unique, short, and friendly URL (slug) for my poll,  
**So that** it looks professional when shared on social media.

### Story 5: QR Code Sharing
**As a** Creator,  
**I want to** download a QR code for my specific poll,  
**So that** I can print it on flyers or display it on screens for easy mobile access.

---

## üó≥Ô∏è Epic: Voting Experience

### Story 6: Seamless Voting
**As a** Voter,  
**I want to** vote on a poll without needing to sign up (optional),  
**So that** the barrier to entry is low.

### Story 7: Mobile Responsiveness
**As a** Voter,  
**I want to** view the poll clearly on my mobile device,  
**So that** I can vote on the go.

---

## üß† Epic: Analytics & AI

### Story 8: View Basic Analytics
**As a** Creator,  
**I want to** see total views, responses, and calculation of response rates,  
**So that** I know how well my poll is performing.

### Story 9: AI Insights
**As a** Creator,  
**I want to** ask the AI questions about my poll results (e.g., "Summarize the feedback"),  
**So that** I can get qualitative insights without manually analyzing every vote.
